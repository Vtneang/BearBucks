Why Bear Bucks

BearBucks visually displays and organizes purchases to help individuals keep track of their personal finances. We also allow multiple users to collaborate on their finances and create shared budgets. We strive to enable users to easily understand their finances through an approachable design and with informative graphics that summarize their data.
